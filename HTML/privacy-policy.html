Privacy Policy

CityReport handles report data with care so residents can raise local issues while understanding how their information is used.

Effective May 29, 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as account details, contact information, report descriptions, issue categories, locations, and uploaded photos. We may also collect basic usage information that helps us keep the service reliable and secure.

How We Use Information

  • To create, display, route, and update community issue reports.
  • To send account, status, support, and safety-related messages.
  • To improve the performance, accessibility, and reliability of CityReport.
  • To prevent spam, abuse, unauthorized access, and misuse of the platform.

Public Report Content

Issue reports may be visible to other residents, city partners, or response teams. Avoid including private personal details in report titles, descriptions, photos, or comments unless those details are necessary for resolving the issue.

Sharing Information

We may share report details with public agencies, service providers, or support partners when needed to operate the platform, resolve an issue, comply with law, or protect the safety and rights of users and the public.

Your Choices

You may update account details, request corrections, or ask about deletion of personal information by contacting support. Some public report records may need to remain available for accountability, legal, safety, or operational reasons.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@cityreport.local.
