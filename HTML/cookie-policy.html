Cookie Policy

This policy explains how CityReport may use cookies and similar technologies to support sign-in, preferences, security, and service improvement.

Effective May 29, 2026

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files stored by your browser. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as whether you are signed in or which preferences you selected.

How We Use Cookies

  • Essential cookies help pages load, forms work, and sessions stay secure.
  • Preference cookies may remember choices such as saved settings or filters.
  • Analytics cookies may help us understand broad usage patterns and improve the service.
  • Security cookies may help detect abuse, spam, or unauthorized activity.

Third-Party Services

Some tools that support CityReport, such as hosting, analytics, maps, or embedded content, may set their own cookies. Their use is governed by their own policies.

Your Controls

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may limit sign-in, reporting, filtering, or other parts of the platform.

Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy when our tools or practices change. The effective date above shows when this page was last revised.

Contact

Questions about cookies can be sent to support@cityreport.local.
