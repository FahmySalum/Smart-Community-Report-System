Accessibility

CityReport is intended to be usable by as many residents as possible, including people who rely on keyboards, assistive technology, captions, contrast, and clear language.

Effective May 29, 2026

Our Commitment

We aim to make core reporting, browsing, and account workflows accessible and understandable. Accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we review the platform as content, features, and standards evolve.

Design Practices

  • Use readable text, clear headings, and consistent navigation.
  • Support keyboard focus states and logical tab order.
  • Maintain sufficient color contrast for important interface elements.
  • Use labels and descriptive text for forms, links, and controls.

Known Limitations

Some prototype content, third-party embeds, maps, or uploaded photos may not yet include complete accessible alternatives. We prioritize fixes that affect reporting, browsing, support, and public issue visibility.

Feedback

If you experience an accessibility barrier, please tell us what happened, which page you were using, and which assistive technology or browser you used if you are comfortable sharing it.

Contact

Accessibility feedback can be sent to support@cityreport.local.
