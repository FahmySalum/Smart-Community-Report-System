Built for cleaner, safer, more responsive neighborhoods.

Our Mission

We help communities turn everyday observations into trackable action. From road damage to lighting, waste, drainage, and public safety concerns, every report becomes part of a shared public record.

How We Work

CityReport keeps reporting simple, organizes issues by urgency and category, and makes progress visible so residents and response teams can stay aligned.

What We Value

Clear records, useful action, public trust

Transparency

Reports stay visible from submission through resolution so the community can understand what is happening.

Accountability

Every case has a status, category, priority, and update trail to make follow-up easier.

Participation

Residents can help surface issues quickly and keep attention on the places they use every day.
